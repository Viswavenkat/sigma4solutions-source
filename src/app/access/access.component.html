Accessibility Service






PDF Accessibility

PDFs are commonly used for sharing and presenting documents, and it's important to make sure that these documents can be effectively navigated, read, and understood by people with a variety of disabilities, including visual, auditory, and motor impairments





Word Accessibility

Word accessibility services pertain to techniques, practices, and tools used to ensure that Microsoft Word documents are accessible to individuals with disabilities.





PPT Accessibility

PPT accessibility refers to the process of creating presentations in Microsoft PPT that are accessible to individuals with disabilities. Creating accessible PPT presentations ensures that all users, including those with visual, auditory, and motor impairments, can effectively engage with the content.





Excel Accessibility

Excel accessibility involves creating spreadsheets in Microsoft Excel that can be effectively navigated and understood by individuals with disabilities. Accessibility in Excel is crucial to ensure that users with visual, auditory, and motor impairments can access and interact with spreadsheet content
